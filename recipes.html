Peachy Mango Smoothie

  • 2 peaches
  • 1 peeled mango
  • 1 frozen banana
  • chilled milk
  • chilled yoghurt

Instructions

Combine all ingredients into blender and blend until smooth.


Tamarind Juice

  • fresh tamarind pods
  • sugar, sweetener, or honey
  • water
  • ice

Instructions

  1. Remove tamarind pulp from pods.
  2. Blend pulp with water in blender for 60 seconds.
  3. Strain mixture into glass jug.
  4. Add sugar and ice.


Baobab Juice

  • baobab seeds
  • sugar, sweetener, or honey
  • water
  • ice

Instructions

  1. Soak baobab seeds in water for a few hours.
  2. Blend mixture in blender for 30 seconds.
  3. Strain mixture into glass jug.
  4. Add sugar and ice.
